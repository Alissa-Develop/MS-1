About

Peace, love & infinity since 2007.


Peace

Founded in 2007, Infinity Yoga Studio is the perfect place to escape the hustle & bustle of everyday life. Our class list includes Yin, Reiki and Restorative, so you can finally feel at peace!

Love

Located in the heart of NYC, Infinity is the only place where you can leave your stress outside while you inhale the love from the around you & exhale that positive energy right back at them.

Harmony

With classes ranging from Yin (or "Restorative" Yoga) to Yang (a more active practice most commonly known as "Vinyasa"), Infinity Studo has all the tools needed to bring harmony & balance to your busy life.
